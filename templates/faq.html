FAQ

What is EmojiSoul?

EmojiSoul is an AI product powered by OpenAI's GPT-3.5 model. It is designed to add the most accurate and relevant emojis to text.

Is EmojiSoul for free?

Yes, it is completely free for everyone!

Which languages does EmojiSoul support?

EmojiSoul supports all languages. But, If the input language is English, you can expect the highest quality output from EmojiSoul. This means that if you enter text in any language, EmojiSoul will generate an output in that language, but the quality of the output may vary.
